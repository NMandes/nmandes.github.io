About

Smite Charts

Smite Chats is a site dedicated to improving the experience of Smite players through the empowerment of visual data. Players can see aggregate data of their favorate characters in a clean and meaningful way.

Gods and their stats are displayed by level to give insights on a characters ingame life cycle. Selecting the God of your choice will give details on how your selecetion stacks up to the rest of the playing field.

With this information we hope that it will aid your descisions and give a well deserved advantage on the battle field.

Made with passion and love in NYC.
